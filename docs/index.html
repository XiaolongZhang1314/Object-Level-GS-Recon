Research on Object-Level Reconstruction Based on 3D Gaussian Models

Xiaolong Zhang   |   Under Review

Abstract

We present an object-level 3D reconstruction framework based on 3D Gaussian Splatting. The proposed method reconstructs individual objects from multi-view observations by optimizing a compact set of 3D Gaussians. By introducing probabilistic object modeling, the method effectively suppresses background interference and produces clean object-level representations.
